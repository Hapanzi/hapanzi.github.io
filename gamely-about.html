What is Gamely

The trivia game you actually control

Gamely flips the script on traditional trivia. Instead of answering someone else's questions, you create your own — then challenge your friends, family, or anyone who dares to take you on. Every game is personal, competitive, and completely yours.

01

Create your questions

Write questions on any topic you like — pop culture, history, football, whatever you know best.

02

Challenge anyone

Send a challenge to friends or go public. Anyone can take on your quiz and put their knowledge to the test.

03

See who wins

Track scores, compare results, and find out once and for all who the real trivia champion is.
